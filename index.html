About

Hi! We are Paul and Chris and together we create the Disturbing Works Studio.

When the Night Falls is our first project, a survival game with a dense horror atmosphere. We have created a randomly generated 2d world of unique digital hand drawing embedded in a 3D world. Our story will take you to a dark forest dominated by horror monsters. The longest night in the forest unleashes dark forces and forces the inhabitants to seek shelter and help. As a Keeper of the Forest, you will try to help the weaker and find a way to restore daylight. You will build a new society in the middle of the dark forest and save them from the evil hidden in the deep forest.

Most of the creatures in the game are influenced by Slovenian myths and stories. We try to create a world known from fairy tales told to children before bedtime, when most families lived in the villages surrounded by a wild dark forest and stimulated the human imagination. Back then, people based their beliefs on nature. There were good spirits and bad spirits in the forest. They were an important part of human life and tradition. A rich and inspiring tradition.
